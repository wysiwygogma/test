OPENING ACCOUNT FOR

INDIVIDUALS

For opening an account an individual must provide the following documents:

  • Copy of passport
  • Application for opening an account in compliance with stated form

OPENING ACCOUNT FOR

LEGAL ENTITLES

For opening an account an individual must provide the following documents:

  • Copy of state registration certificate,
  • Copy of charter,
  • Copy of resolution on appointment of director (CEO) or statement from that resolution,
  • Of the entity also has another authorized person, also document verifying authorities of that person,
  • Copies of passports of director (CEO) and authorized persons (if any),
  • Application for opening an account in compliance with stated form,
  • Other documents and information indicated by rules of DIMENSION OJSC
